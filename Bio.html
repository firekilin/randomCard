Backstory (aka The Real Arc No One Saw Coming)

I never really fit in.
Not at school. Not at home. Not even in the spiritual aisle at Target.
Everyone else seemed to settle into life like it was a pre-written script.
I was out here pressing ✖️ on every default role the simulation threw at me.
The good girl. The bad girl. The helper. The hustler. The healer.
None of them felt like me—not until I glitched out and broke the whole system.
I rebelled just to feel something real.
Laughed off the ache.
Muted my softness.
Cycled through identities like tabs left open for too long.
But the real shift?
It wasn’t some neon cosmic awakening—it was a glitch in the matrix.
A weird click sound between your bones.
The sacred nonsense: a past life reading, a cosmic breadcrumb—
and suddenly I saw a glimpse of that other layer.
So I jumped.
Heart trembling, eyes wide, playlist on shuffle.
Then came the real work:
Getting radically curious about who I am underneath it all.
